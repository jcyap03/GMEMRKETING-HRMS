Payroll & Payslip

Approved claims from the same expense month are added to the employee's total payment as reimbursement. They are shown separately and are not included in statutory salary calculations.

Statutory contribution basis: Basic Salary + Fixed Allowance + Commission / OT + Bonus / Adjustment. Outstation allowance and approved claim reimbursements are excluded. The displayed statutory amounts are estimates generated by this offline tool; verify against the latest official contribution schedules before submission.
